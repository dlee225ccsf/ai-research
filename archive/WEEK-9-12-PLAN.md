## Day 57 (Monday) — Interview Materials Pack
**Time: 2.5 hours**

### Morning (1.5 hours)
- [ ] Finalize `Resume-v3` for interview stage

  - Place both deployed projects near top
  - Add direct links to GitHub and live demos
- [ ] Create `INTERVIEW-PACK.md` with:
  - 30-second intro
  - 2-minute "about me"

### Afternoon (1 hour)

## If Market Is Still Slow After Week 12
- Keep same rhythm in 4-week sprints
- Add one credibility lever:
  - open-source contributions (20+ commits/month)
  - one niche specialization (cloud, data, security, or distributed systems)
  - one additional advanced project
- Continue referral-first strategy

This keeps you competitive until timing aligns with opportunity.
